Privacy Policy

Royal CV Maker (the “App”) is published by Digital Frontier Applications Studio.
Last updated: 26 April 2026

Summary We use your information to run the App (accounts, your CV content, optional account profile photo). Some data is processed by Google Firebase. Optional “Remember me” stores your email on the device only. PDF export may load fonts over the internet (for example from Google Fonts). We do not sell your personal information. Contact us for privacy questions or deletion requests.

2. What the App does

The App lets you create, edit, and manage one or more CVs, preview them, and generate PDFs. It may use a user account and stores CV data on your device for quick access.

3. Information we may process

  • Account and sign-in — If you register, we may process your email address, display name, a user identifier, and credentials needed for authentication. Sign-in may be provided through services such as Google Firebase Authentication.
  • CV content you enter — For example your name, contact details, work history, education, and skills, so the App can show and export your CV.
  • Account profile photo (optional) — If you add a photo in your account profile, the file may be stored using Google Firebase Storage and linked from your account (for example as a photo URL).
  • Images in your CV (optional) — A picture you attach to a CV is kept on your device as part of your CV data (for example as a local file path). Only the separate account profile photo flow uploads an image to Firebase Storage.
  • Remember me (optional) — If you enable it at sign-in, your email address may be saved on the device only (for example in app preferences) so the login form can be pre-filled. This is separate from sending credentials when you actually sign in.
  • Data on your device — CV data and related preferences may be stored locally (for example with app-managed storage such as SharedPreferences) so the App works offline where possible. It stays on the device unless a feature explicitly uploads it.

4. How we use this information

We use it to provide the App, maintain your account, generate PDFs, and respond when you contact us. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Service providers and locations

We use service providers to host, authenticate, and store data. As of this policy date, the App uses Google Firebase Authentication and Google Firebase Storage for the features described above. We do not use Firebase Cloud Firestore to store your CVs or similar content in the version of the App covered by this policy. Providers may process data in the United States and other countries where they operate.

When you export a PDF, the App may download font files over the internet (for example via Google Fonts or infrastructure used by our PDF libraries) so text and Arabic script render correctly. Those requests are handled by the relevant provider under its own terms and privacy notices; they are not used by us to identify you beyond standard network operations.

6. How long we keep data

We keep information while your account is active and as needed to provide the App, or longer if the law requires. You may request account deletion or ask questions by email; we will respond in line with applicable law.

7. Your choices

You may be able to update your profile, sign out, or request deletion, depending on the feature. Uninstalling the App may leave local data on the device until you clear app data in system settings.

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards, including protecting data in transit with encryption where the services we use support it. No system is perfectly secure.

9. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). If you believe we have data from a child, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. If you keep using the App after an update, you accept the revised policy where the law allows.
