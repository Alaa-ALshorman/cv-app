2. What the App does

The App lets you create and edit a curriculum vitae (CV), preview it, and generate PDFs. It may use a user account and can store CV data on your device for quick access.

3. Information we may process

  • Account and sign-in — If you register, we may process your email address, display name, a user identifier, and credentials needed for authentication. Sign-in may be provided through services such as Google Firebase Authentication.
  • CV content you enter — For example your name, contact details, work history, education, and skills, so the App can show and export your CV.
  • Profile photo (optional) — If you add a photo, the file or a link to it may be stored using Google Firebase Storage and associated with your profile.
  • Data on your device — CV data may be stored locally (for example in app storage) to improve performance and usage without a network. It stays on the device unless a feature explicitly uploads it.

4. How we use this information

We use it to provide the App, maintain your account, generate PDFs, and respond when you contact us. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Service providers and locations

We use service providers to host, authenticate, and store data, including Google Firebase. They may process data in the United States and other countries where they operate.

6. How long we keep data

We keep information while your account is active and as needed to provide the App, or longer if the law requires. You may request account deletion or ask questions by email; we will respond in line with applicable law.

7. Your choices

You may be able to update your profile, sign out, or request deletion, depending on the feature. Uninstalling the App may leave local data on the device until you clear app data in system settings.

8. Security

9. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). If you believe we have data from a child, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.
